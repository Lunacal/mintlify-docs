---
title: 'Embed Your Event In Mails'
description: 'Embed scheduling options in emails to let recipients easily select and confirm meeting times.'
mode: 'wide'
---

## Step 1

To begin, navigate to your list of available event types, such as a 30-minute meeting. Click the settings button and select the "Embed" option from the dropdown menu.

<Frame><img src="/images/embed-in-mail/image1.jpg" /></Frame>

## Step 2

Next, you will be presented with two options: "Add to Website" and "Add to Email." Click on "Add to Email" to proceed to the relevant page.

<Frame><img src="/images/embed-in-mail/image2.jpg" /></Frame>

## Step 3

On this page, choose the dates and times you wish to share. For example, you can select the 14th with time slots at 9:00, 11:00, and 12:00.

<Frame><img src="/images/embed-in-mail/image3.jpg" /></Frame>

## Step 4

For the 15th, select time slots such as 15:00 and 16:00, and Copy the provided code.

<Frame><img src="/images/embed-in-mail/image5.png" /></Frame>

## Step 5

Paste it into the body of your email as shown.

<Frame><img src="/images/embed-in-mail/image6.jpg" /></Frame>

## Step 6

Recipients of the email will be able to click on the provided time slots to select their preferences.

<Frame><img src="/images/embed-in-mail/image7.jpg" /></Frame>

## Step 7

Upon clicking a time slot, recipients will need to enter their name and email address and then click "Confirm."

<Frame><img src="/images/embed-in-mail/image8.jpg" /></Frame>
