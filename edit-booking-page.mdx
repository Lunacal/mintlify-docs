# Edit Your Booking Page

This article will guide you through the process of editing your booking page, adding and modifying widgets, and customizing the appearance of your page.

---

![Step 1](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/.../8eb161af-0e4a-4b0d-a0a9-d1aa2849fc4d.png)

This will redirect you to your booking page.
