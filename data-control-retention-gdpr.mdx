## Overview

lunacal is committed to protecting personal data and complying with the **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)**.

This section explains:

- What data Lunacal stores
- Who can request access or deletion
- How long data is retained
- How to submit a GDPR request

You can find this section at:

**Settings → General → Data Control & Retention (GDPR)** or directly via: `https://app.lunacal.ai/new-settings?tabName=general`

## Your information (individual users)

If you use Lunacal as an individual user, you have the right to:

- Request access to the personal data we hold about you
- Request deletion of your personal data

### Data covered

This may include:

- Account details
- Booking history
- Any other data associated with your lunacal account

### How to make a request

Send an email to [**hi@lunacal.live**](mailto:hi@lunacal.live) from the email address linked to your Lunacal account. This helps us verify your identity and process your request securely.

## Your team’s information (team owners)

If you are a **team owner**, you can request access to or deletion of your team’s data.

### Data covered

This may include:

- Team bookings
- Team member profiles
- Team-level settings and configurations

### Important notes

- Only **verified team owners** can submit requests for team data
- Team data is retained while the team account is active
- Once a deletion request is confirmed, team data is deleted within **30 days**

## Booker information (your guests)

Bookers (or hosts submitting on their behalf) can request access to or deletion of booking-related data.

### Data covered

This may include:

- Booker name
- Contact information
- Event details (date, time, event type)

### Submitting a request for a booker

If you are submitting a request on behalf of a booker, please include identifying details such as:

- Event link
- Booking timestamp

Booker data is retained as long as the event exists and can be deleted earlier upon request.

## Request processing & retention

- All valid GDPR requests are processed within **30 days**
- In certain cases, data may be retained longer if required by:
  - Law or regulatory obligations
  - Security, abuse prevention, or fraud detection

## Account deletion

You can permanently delete your lunacal account from this page.

### Important

- **Account deletion is irreversible**
- All associated data will be scheduled for deletion according to GDPR guidelines

Before deleting your account, make sure you have exported or saved any data you may need in the future.

If you have any questions about data privacy or GDPR, feel free to contact the Lunacal team at [**hi@lunacal.live**](mailto:hi@lunacal.live).
