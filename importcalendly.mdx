# Seamless Integration of Calendly Settings

> This document provides a concise guide on how to import your Calendly settings into the Lunacal app. Follow these steps to seamlessly synchronize your booking schedules and make the most of your productivity tools.

### Step 1

Begin by verifying that there are no upcoming bookings visible in the booking section, which indicates that the calendar is ready for import.

<Frame><img src="/images/importCalendly/image1.jpg" /></Frame>

### Step 2

Ensure that you are signed into Calendly with your correct account credentials to proceed with the integration.

<Frame><img src="/images/importCalendly/image2.jpg" /></Frame>

### Step 3

Navigate to the settings section within the Lunacal app to access integration options.

<Frame><img src="/images/importCalendly/image3.jpg" /></Frame>

### Step 4

Locate and select the "Import Calendly" setting. Click on "Connect Calendly" to initiate the import process.

<Frame><img src="/images/importCalendly/image4.jpg" /></Frame>

### Step 5

Once the import is complete, navigate back to the booking section where your Calendly bookings should now be visible, confirming a successful integration.

<Frame><img src="/images/importCalendly/image5.jpg" /></Frame>
