What ‚ÄúDisable Cancellation / Rescheduling‚Äù Means

- By default, clients may be allowed to cancel or reschedule their booked events. With these settings:

‚Äú**Disable Cancelling**‚Äù ‚Äî prevents clients from canceling once a booking is confirmed.

‚Äú**Disable Rescheduling**‚Äù ‚Äî prevents clients from changing the time/date of a confirmed booking.

## Step 1  

Go to [Event Types](https://app.lunacal.ai/event-types).  

<Frame><img src="/images/Cancellation-Resheduling/CR1.webp" /></Frame>

---

## Step 2  

Select the event for which you want to disable **cancellation** or **rescheduling** and click on the Settings icon on that event card.  

<Frame><img src="/images/Cancellation-Resheduling/CR2.webp" /></Frame>

---

## Step 3  

Choose **Edit** from the dropdown.  

<Frame><img src="/images/Cancellation-Resheduling/CR3.webp" /></Frame>

---

## Step 4  

In the "**Event Types**" section, scroll down to find "**Advanced.**"  

<Frame><img src="/images/Cancellation-Resheduling/CR4.webp" /></Frame>

---

## Step 5  

"**Disable Cancelling**" ‚Üí Toggle this **ON** to prevent the booker from cancelling the meeting.  

<Frame><img src="/images/Cancellation-Resheduling/CR5.webp" /></Frame>

---

## Step 6  

"**Disable Rescheduling**" ‚Üí Toggle this **ON** to prevent the booker from rescheduling the meeting.  

<Frame><img src="/images/Cancellation-Resheduling/CR6.webp" /></Frame>

---

## Step 7  

Click "**Save**" to apply the changes.  

<Frame><img src="/images/Cancellation-Resheduling/CR7.webp" /></Frame>

üîÑ **What Happens to Existing Bookings & How It Affects Clients**

- For new bookings after you enable the toggle: Clients will not see options to cancel or reschedule.

- For existing bookings (before you enabled the toggle): They remain unaffected and attendee can reschedule/cancel bookings.

- Clients trying to cancel/reschedule: They will not have the option on their booking page (or the links/buttons will be disabled).

- If a booking needs to be rescheduled/cancelled by you (**the host**), you can still do it via your [bookings](https://app.lunacal.ai/bookings/upcoming).
