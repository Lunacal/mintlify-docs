---
title: "How to Add or Remove Team Members"
description: "Learn how to add new members to your Lunacal team or remove existing members from a team."
---

## Adding Team Members

Step 1\
Navigate to the **Teams** section in your Lunacal dashboard.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Adding-Removing/TC1.webp)
</Frame>

---

Step 2\
Select the specific **team/company** you want to manage.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Adding-Removing/TC2.webp)
</Frame>

---

Step 3\
Click on the settings icon and then choose **“Invite team member”**

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Adding-Removing/TC3.webp)
</Frame>

---

Step 4\
Enter the email address (or multiple addresses) of the members you want to add.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Adding-Removing/TC4.webp)
</Frame>
---

Step 5\
The user receives an **invitation email.**

<Frame>
  ![](/images/creating_team/team6.webp)
</Frame>
---

Step 6\
If they are not already on Lunacal, they need to click **“Create your account”** in the invitation email.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/creating_team/team7.webp)
</Frame>
---

Step 7\
After clicking **“Create your account,”** they just need to complete the sign-up form to join Lunacal.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/creating_team/team8.webp)
</Frame>
---

Step 8\
Important: After creating their account, the user must go to the **Teams** section and click **“Accept”** to officially join the team.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/creating_team/team9.webp)
</Frame>

---

## Removing Team Members

Step 9\
Click on the **Settings** icon and choose **“Team Settings.”**

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Adding-Removing/TC10.webp)
</Frame>

---

Step 10\
Scroll down to the **Team Members** section.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Adding-Removing/TC11.webp)
</Frame>
---

Step 11\
Click the **three dots** next to the member you want to remove, then select **“Remove.”**

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Adding-Removing/TC12.webp)
</Frame>

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Adding-Removing/TC13.webp)
</Frame>
