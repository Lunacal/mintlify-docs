---

## Step 1

To configure your out of office settings, click on "Settings", Scroll down to the "Out of Office" section and click on "Add."

<Frame><img src="/images/out_of_office/1.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 2

Select the dates during which you will be unavailable. e.g. i'm unavailable from 1 July to 10 July.

<Frame><img src="/images/out_of_office/2.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 3

If you're part of any team, you can select the member to forward your meeting when you're on leave.

<Frame><img src="/images/out_of_office/3.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 4

Click on the "Create" button.

<Frame><img src="/images/out_of_office/4.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 5

Your out of office is now set up.

<Frame><img src="/images/out_of_office/5.webp" /></Frame>
