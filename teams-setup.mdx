## Step 1

Navigate to Teams Section and Click on "Create New Team".

<Frame>
  ![](/images/teams_setup/1.webp)
</Frame>

## Step 2

Enter the name of your team, For e.g. "Sales North America", then Click on Continue.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/teams_setup/2.webp)
</Frame>

## Step 3

Click "Add Team Member", This takes you to a page to invite team members.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/teams_setup/3.webp)
</Frame>

## Step 4

Enter the email, click **Send Invite**.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/teams_setup/4.webp)
</Frame>

## Step 5

User Gets an Invitation Email.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/teams_setup/5.webp)
</Frame>

## Step 6

Click on "Finish" button.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/teams_setup/6.webp)
</Frame>

## Step 7

Your team is ready

<Frame>
  ![](/images/teams_setup/7.webp)
</Frame>
