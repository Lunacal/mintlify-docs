## Step 1

To embed your event type into your website, start by clicking on the settings button.

<Frame><img src="/images/embed_in_web/image1.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 2

Next, click on the "Embed" option.

<Frame><img src="/images/embed_in_web/image2.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 3

Then, select "Add to Website."

<Frame><img src="/images/embed_in_web/image3.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 4

Proceed by copying the provided HTML code.

<Frame><img src="/images/embed_in_web/image4.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 5

Using this HTML code, your development team can embed the event or calendar into your website. e.g, you can see it on lunacal.ai "Contact Us" Page.

<Frame><img src="/images/embed_in_web/embed1.webp" /></Frame>
