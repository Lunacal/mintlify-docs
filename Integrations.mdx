# Integrating and Managing Applications

> This document provides a comprehensive overview of how to integrate and manage various applications within the platform. Follow these steps to navigate through integration options, set up new applications, and manage existing ones effectively.

### Step 1

Navigate to the Integrations tab.

<Frame><img src="/images/integrationSetup/image1.jpg" /></Frame>

### Step 2

Explore the available categories of integration, including Conferencing, Calendar, Automation, CRM, Messaging, and Payment. Each category contains several applications ready for integration. To view options, click on Conferencing.

<Frame><img src="/images/integrationSetup/image2.jpg" /></Frame>

### Step 3

Within the Conferencing category, you will find applications such as FaceTime, Google Meet, MS Teams, and Zoom Video. In the Calendar section, available calendars include Apple Calendar, Google Calendar, and Outlook Calendar.

<Frame><img src="/images/integrationSetup/image3.jpg" /></Frame>

### Step 4

For Automation, Zapier is available. In the CRM category, you can integrate with HubSpot CRM and Pipedrive CRM.

<Frame><img src="/images/integrationSetup/image4.jpg" /></Frame>

### Step 5

In Messaging, WhatsApp is available for integration, while in the Payment category, PayPal is offered.

<Frame><img src="/images/integrationSetup/image5.jpg" /></Frame>

### Step 6

Set up PayPal integration with your event types. To remove any applications, simply click on Installed Apps.

<Frame><img src="/images/integrationSetup/image6.jpg" /></Frame>

### Step 7

If you wish to remove an application, such as Google Meet, click on the app and select 'Remove App.'

<Frame><img src="/images/integrationSetup/image7.jpg" /></Frame>

### Step 8

This process allows you to uninstall applications.

<Frame><img src="/images/integrationSetup/image8.jpg" /></Frame>
