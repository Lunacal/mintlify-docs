---
title: "Setup Workflows"
description: "Use “Workflows” to automate notifications for your events—set a trigger, pick recipients, and send a template. Keep steps the same; only fill in names, timing, and recipients."
mode: "wide"
---

## Step 1

Go to the “Workflows” tab and click “Create New Workflow”.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/setup_workflows/1.webp)
</Frame>

## Step 2

Provide a unique name for your workflow in the designated field.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/setup_workflows/2.webp)
</Frame>

## Step 3

Determine the event type to which the workflow will apply. For instance i'm selecting "Meeting"

<Frame>
  ![](/images/setup_workflows/3.webp)
</Frame>

## Step 4

Choose when the workflow should be triggered

<Frame>
  ![](/images/setup_workflows/4.webp)
</Frame>

## Step 5

Pick a trigger: “Before an event starts”, “When event is canceled”, “When new event is booked”, “After event ends”, or “When event is rescheduled”.\
Example: Send a reminder “Before an event starts” so attendees don’t miss the call.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/setup_workflows/5.webp)
</Frame>

## Step 6

If using a “Before an event starts” trigger, set the time (e.g., 2 Hours).\
Example: For first-time “Meeting” calls, set 24 Hours so people prep earlier.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/setup_workflows/6.webp)
</Frame>

## Step 7

Specify the recipients of the workflow trigger.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/setup_workflows/7.webp)
</Frame>

## Step 8

Select “attendees”, “host”, or add a specific email.\
Example: Add your coordinator’s email so they get alerts for last-minute changes.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/setup_workflows/10.webp)
</Frame>

## Step 9

Add another action if needed.\
Example: After a reschedule, send a follow-up to both the “host” and “attendees” confirming the new time.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/setup_workflows/9.webp)
</Frame>

## Step 10

Use the default “reminder” email template or customize it.\
Example: Personalize the subject with {EVENT_NAME} to make the email clear at a glance.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/setup_workflows/11.webp)
</Frame>

## Step 11

Insert variables as needed (e.g., {ATTENDEE_NAME}, {EVENT_TIME}, {LOCATION_LINK}).\
Example: Include {LOCATION_LINK} so Zoom/Meet joins are one click

<Frame>
  ![](/images/setup_workflows/12.webp)
</Frame>

## Step 12

Click “Save” to activate the workflow.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/setup_workflows/13.webp)
</Frame>
