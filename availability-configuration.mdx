## Step 1

To begin configuring availability, navigate to the availability section. 

<Frame><img src="/images/setup_availability/1.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 2

You will notice a default schedule that the app has automatically created for you. To create a new availability entry, click the "Create New Availability" button 

<Frame><img src="/images/setup_availability/2.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 3

Enter the name, such as "Introduction" and click on "Continue" button.

<Frame><img src="/images/setup_availability/3.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 4

lunacal sets a default schedule, typically from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. You can modify this schedule by adjusting the times and enabling or disabling specific days according to your needs.

<Frame><img src="/images/setup_availability/4.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 5

You have the option to add new time slots. For instance, if you are available from 8:00 AM to 11:30 AM, you can easily include this in your schedule.

<Frame><img src="/images/setup_availability/5.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 6

Additionally, you can specify other time slots, such as from 12:00 PM to 2:00 PM.

<Frame><img src="/images/setup_availability/6.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 7

You may copy these timings to other available days, such as Thursday and Friday.

<Frame><img src="/images/setup_availability/7.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 8

Once your schedule is set, click on "Apply."

<Frame><img src="/images/setup_availability/8.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 9

To add an override, Click on "Add an override" button. 

<Frame><img src="/images/setup_availability/9.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 10
for example, if you are unavailable on the 13th, select the date, enable "Mark Unavailable," click "Add Override," close the window, and your override will be set.

<Frame><img src="/images/setup_availability/10.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 11

You can adjust the time zone settings as needed, and choose to set this availability as the default if desired.

<Frame><img src="/images/setup_availability/11.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 12

After making all necessary changes, click the "Save" button.

<Frame><img src="/images/setup_availability/12.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 13

Your availability configuration is now complete.

<Frame><img src="/images/setup_availability/13.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 14

You can also configure availability settings within your event types,By navigating to the event types section.

<Frame><img src="/images/setup_availability/14.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 15

Select the Event, click on "Availability," and use the dropdown to apply the newly configured availability.

<Frame><img src="/images/setup_availability/15.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 16

Review your settings and click "Save."

<Frame><img src="/images/setup_availability/16.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 17

The process is now complete.

<Frame><img src="/images/setup_availability/17.webp" /></Frame>
