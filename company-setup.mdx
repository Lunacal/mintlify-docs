---
title: "Using Lunacal with Different Companies"
description: "Learn how to set up multiple company teams in Lunacal and manage team members for each company."
mode: "wide"
---
 
Step 1  
Navigate to the "Teams Section" and click on “Create a New Team.”

<Frame><img src="/images/company-setup/CT1.webp" /></Frame>

Step 2  
Enter the name of your company, for example “Company 1”, then click "Continue". 

<Frame><img src="/images/company-setup/CT2.webp" /></Frame>


Step 3  
Enter the email address (or multiple addresses) of the team members you want to add.  

<Frame><img src="/images/company-setup/CT3.webp" /></Frame>



Step 4  
The user receives an invitation email.  

<Frame><img src="/images/company-setup/CT4.webp" /></Frame>


Step 5  
If they are not already on Lunacal, they need to click “Create your account” to join your team.  

<Frame><img src="/images/company-setup/CT5.webp" /></Frame>



Step 6  
After clicking “Create your account,” they just need to complete the sign-up form to join Lunacal.  

<Frame><img src="/images/company-setup/CT6.webp" /></Frame>



Step 7  
Important: After creating their account, the user must go to the **Teams** section and click “Accept” to join the team.  

<Frame><img src="/images/company-setup/CT7.webp" /></Frame>

# How to Add Another Company

Step 8  
Again, go to "Teams" → “Create a New Team.” 

<Frame><img src="/images/company-setup/CT8.webp" /></Frame>



Step 9  
Enter the name of your other company, for example “Company 2”, then click Continue. 

<Frame><img src="/images/company-setup/CT9.webp" /></Frame>



Step 10  
Invite only the members you want to be part of this specific company.  

<Frame><img src="/images/company-setup/CT10.webp" /></Frame>


Step 11  
The user receives an invitation email.  

<Frame><img src="/images/company-setup/CT4.webp" /></Frame>

Step 12

If they are not already on Lunacal, they need to click “Create your account” to join your team.  

<Frame><img src="/images/company-setup/CT5.webp" /></Frame>

Step 13

After clicking “Create your account,” they just need to complete the sign-up form to join Lunacal.  

<Frame><img src="/images/company-setup/CT6.webp" /></Frame>

Step 14 
Important: After creating their account, the user must go to the "Teams" section and click “Accept” to join the team.  

<Frame><img src="/images/company-setup/CT7.webp" /></Frame>

Step 15  
Now both company teams are set up.  

<Frame><img src="/images/company-setup/CT12.webp" /></Frame>


Step 16  
Each member added to a specific company team can create their own events from their individual profile under "Event Types".  

<Frame><img src="/images/company-setup/CT13.webp" /></Frame>


Step 17  
For guidance on using the Teams setup, please refer to the **Teams Setup** section in the help docs.
