---
title: "Adding Calendars to Lunacal"
description: "Learn how to integrate your calendar with Lunacal.ai to sync availability and automatically add bookings."
mode: 'wide'
---

**Step 1:** Begin by navigating to your settings.

![Step 1](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/ebbf25da-f06e-4e91-8b05-b5f8e12127c5/b52a4b2c-9b9c-466b-a08e-6d4c896013dc/04933a65-b95f-4907-b4a1-0c9461e8895a/images/03aa0b77-5840-475d-a1f1-0692d11ff6f9.png)

---

**Step 2:** Click on the **Calendar** option.

![Step 2](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/ebbf25da-f06e-4e91-8b05-b5f8e12127c5/b52a4b2c-9b9c-466b-a08e-6d4c896013dc/04933a65-b95f-4907-b4a1-0c9461e8895a/images/d9ef9fba-f6e9-4c71-bf22-37a54572f1ae.png)

You'll notice that there's no calendar connected at this point.

---

**Step 3:** Click on **Connect**.

![Step 3](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/ebbf25da-f06e-4e91-8b05-b5f8e12127c5/b52a4b2c-9b9c-466b-a08e-6d4c896013dc/04933a65-b95f-4907-b4a1-0c9461e8895a/images/ca940270-ddf1-42a9-8e3a-5b796d9b0740.png)

---

**Step 4:** Choose **Calendar Apps**. For this tutorial, we're connecting a Google Calendar. Click on **Install**.

![Step 4](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/ebbf25da-f06e-4e91-8b05-b5f8e12127c5/b52a4b2c-9b9c-466b-a08e-6d4c896013dc/04933a65-b95f-4907-b4a1-0c9461e8895a/images/3536c0c9-c2be-448a-b3cb-d561ab508f17.png)

---

**Step 5:** Select your Google account.

![Step 5](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/ebbf25da-f06e-4e91-8b05-b5f8e12127c5/b52a4b2c-9b9c-466b-a08e-6d4c896013dc/04933a65-b95f-4907-b4a1-0c9461e8895a/images/4576a324-693f-49a8-bc5f-6cf96eda6ace.png)

---

**Step 6:** Click on **Continue**.

![Step 6-1](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/ebbf25da-f06e-4e91-8b05-b5f8e12127c5/b52a4b2c-9b9c-466b-a08e-6d4c896013dc/04933a65-b95f-4907-b4a1-0c9461e8895a/images/6a044f5b-47b0-441d-83ac-a117d832298c.png)

![Step 6-2](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/ebbf25da-f06e-4e91-8b05-b5f8e12127c5/b52a4b2c-9b9c-466b-a08e-6d4c896013dc/04933a65-b95f-4907-b4a1-0c9461e8895a/images/3604b498-0e60-46fe-ad47-9c501e809600.png)

Your calendar is now connected. You'll see that events are being added to this calendar.

---

**Step 7:** If you have multiple calendars, you're able to change the calendar where events are added. For example, if you have another calendar named "Calendar 2.0", you can switch to this one.

![Step 7-1](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/ebbf25da-f06e-4e91-8b05-b5f8e12127c5/b52a4b2c-9b9c-466b-a08e-6d4c896013dc/04933a65-b95f-4907-b4a1-0c9461e8895a/images/ce4e2bd0-3941-451f-b2d1-fe554229bb94.png)

Click to change the calendar where events are added.

![Step 7-2](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/ebbf25da-f06e-4e91-8b05-b5f8e12127c5/b52a4b2c-9b9c-466b-a08e-6d4c896013dc/04933a65-b95f-4907-b4a1-0c9461e8895a/images/7da53f2f-05a6-42e0-a657-7508b579144e.png)

You'll see that the calendar for adding events has now changed to "Calendar 2.0".

---

**Step 8:** You also have the option to add other calendars. For example, you might want to add another Google Calendar or install new calendar apps like Apple Calendar or Outlook Calendar.

![Step 8](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/ebbf25da-f06e-4e91-8b05-b5f8e12127c5/b52a4b2c-9b9c-466b-a08e-6d4c896013dc/04933a65-b95f-4907-b4a1-0c9461e8895a/images/d74f7082-ae50-475a-941b-98ef90801fdb.png)
