## Step 1

Navigate to the Integrations tab.

<Frame><img src="/images/Integrations/2.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 2

Explore the available categories of integration, including Conferencing, Calendar, Automation, CRM, Messaging, and Payment. Each category contains several applications ready for integration. To view options, click on Conferencing.

<Frame><img src="/images/Integrations/1.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 3

Within the Conferencing category, you will find applications such as FaceTime, Google Meet, MS Teams, and Zoom Video. In the Calendar section, available calendars include Apple Calendar, Google Calendar, and Outlook Calendar.

<Frame><img src="/images/Integrations/3.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 4

For Automation, Zapier is available. In the CRM category, you can integrate with HubSpot CRM and Pipedrive CRM.

<Frame><img src="/images/Integrations/4.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 5

In Messaging, WhatsApp is available for integration.

<Frame><img src="/images/Integrations/5.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 6

In the Payment category, you'll see PayPal.

<Frame><img src="/images/Integrations/6.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 7
To remove any applications, simply click on Installed Apps, click on 3 dotes and then Click on 'Remove App.' This process allows you to uninstall applications.

<Frame><img src="/images/Integrations/7.webp" /></Frame>
