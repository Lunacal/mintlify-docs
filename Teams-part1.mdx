## Step 3: Name Your Team

In this example, we're naming it "Sales North America".

![Name Team](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/2fbb8174-fc18-4f55-a7bb-cb4cbd83c515/d99445d3-d0e7-4a4e-9ff1-94dea986ef50/91ef46d1-d0b8-4b9e-96f2-6351d7880d34/images/26da2fce-6c85-4c3d-9097-1adb154569a7.png)

## Step 4: Click "Continue"

This takes you to a page to invite team members.

![Continue](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/2fbb8174-fc18-4f55-a7bb-cb4cbd83c515/d99445d3-d0e7-4a4e-9ff1-94dea986ef50/91ef46d1-d0b8-4b9e-96f2-6351d7880d34/images/9f666b8b-504f-46c6-86da-1c761efbff27.png)

## Step 5: Click "Add Team Member"

![Add Team Member](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/2fbb8174-fc18-4f55-a7bb-cb4cbd83c515/d99445d3-d0e7-4a4e-9ff1-94dea986ef50/91ef46d1-d0b8-4b9e-96f2-6351d7880d34/images/93dd63f1-cc07-4623-bceb-8e2dc0938e4e.png)

## Booking Page Overview

You’ll now see the team’s booking page with members listed. You can book individual meetings, or create round robin or collective events.
