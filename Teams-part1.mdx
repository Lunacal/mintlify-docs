## Step 1

Navigate to Teams Section and Click on "Create New Team".

![Create a Team](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/2fbb8174-fc18-4f55-a7bb-cb4cbd83c515/d99445d3-d0e7-4a4e-9ff1-94dea986ef50/91ef46d1-d0b8-4b9e-96f2-6351d7880d34/images/0b6f2c8f-30a2-423f-9404-53df6b8cd79d.png)

## Step 2 

Enter the name of your team.

![Create New Team](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/2fbb8174-fc18-4f55-a7bb-cb4cbd83c515/d99445d3-d0e7-4a4e-9ff1-94dea986ef50/91ef46d1-d0b8-4b9e-96f2-6351d7880d34/images/f0fa9f75-4102-473b-8e37-10adfc9ae02b.png)

## Step 3 

For e.g. "Sales North America", then Click on Continue.

![Name Team](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/2fbb8174-fc18-4f55-a7bb-cb4cbd83c515/d99445d3-d0e7-4a4e-9ff1-94dea986ef50/91ef46d1-d0b8-4b9e-96f2-6351d7880d34/images/26da2fce-6c85-4c3d-9097-1adb154569a7.png)

## Step 4 

Click "Add Team Member", This takes you to a page to invite team members.

![Continue](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/2fbb8174-fc18-4f55-a7bb-cb4cbd83c515/d99445d3-d0e7-4a4e-9ff1-94dea986ef50/91ef46d1-d0b8-4b9e-96f2-6351d7880d34/images/9f666b8b-504f-46c6-86da-1c761efbff27.png)

## Step 5 

Enter the email, press comma, and click **Send Invite**.

<Frame><img src="/images/TeamsPart1/invite.png" /></Frame>

## Step 6

User Gets an Invitation Email

![Email Invite](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/2fbb8174-fc18-4f55-a7bb-cb4cbd83c515/d99445d3-d0e7-4a4e-9ff1-94dea986ef50/91ef46d1-d0b8-4b9e-96f2-6351d7880d34/images/633601ef-12f7-4e0e-945e-4392914caca2.png)

## Step 7

Click on "Finish" button.

![Finish](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/2fbb8174-fc18-4f55-a7bb-cb4cbd83c515/d99445d3-d0e7-4a4e-9ff1-94dea986ef50/91ef46d1-d0b8-4b9e-96f2-6351d7880d34/images/027596ff-e4ec-4998-a910-7bf15807baa1.png)

## Step 8

Your team is ready

![Team Ready](https://usercontent.in.prod.clueso.io/2fbb8174-fc18-4f55-a7bb-cb4cbd83c515/d99445d3-d0e7-4a4e-9ff1-94dea986ef50/91ef46d1-d0b8-4b9e-96f2-6351d7880d34/images/7a50847e-f64e-4ad4-ac97-3a279cab2897.png)
