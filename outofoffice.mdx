# Setting Up Out of Office

> This document outlines the steps required to configure your out of office settings. Following these instructions will enable you to inform others of your absence during a specified period.

### Step 1

To configure your out of office settings, click on "My Settings."

<Frame><img src="/images/OutOfOffice/image1.jpg" /></Frame>

### Step 2

Scroll down to the "Out of Office" section and click on "Add."

<Frame><img src="/images/OutOfOffice/image2.jpg" /></Frame>

### Step 3

Select the dates during which you will be unavailable.

<Frame><img src="/images/OutOfOffice/image3.jpg" /></Frame>

### Step 4

For example, choose the dates from May 5th to May 8th.

<Frame><img src="/images/OutOfOffice/image4.jpg" /></Frame>

### Step 5

Select a reason for your absence, such as "Sick."

<Frame><img src="/images/OutOfOffice/image5.jpg" /></Frame>

### Step 6

If desired, provide an additional note.

<Frame><img src="/images/OutOfOffice/image6.jpg" /></Frame>

### Step 7

Click on the "Create" button.

<Frame><img src="/images/OutOfOffice/image7.jpg" /></Frame>

### Step 8

Your out of office notification is now set up.

<Frame><img src="/images/OutOfOffice/image8.jpg" /></Frame>
