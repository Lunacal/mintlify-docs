# Embedding an Event Type in Your Website

> This document provides a concise guide on how to embed an event type into your website. Follow these steps to integrate your event seamlessly, allowing visitors to easily book demos or access your event calendar directly from your site.

### Step 1

To embed your event type into your website, start by clicking on the settings button.

### Step 2

Next, click on the "Embed" option.

<Frame><img src="/images/embedEventWebsite/image2.jpg" /></Frame>

### Step 3

Then, select "Add to Website."
