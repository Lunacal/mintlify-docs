### Step 1

Begin by navigating to the integration section to set up PayPal for your events.

<Frame><img src="/images/paypalSetup/image1.jpg" /></Frame>

### Step 2

Select the "Payment" option.

<Frame><img src="/images/paypalSetup/image2.jpg" /></Frame>

### Step 3

Proceed by clicking the "Install" button.

<Frame><img src="/images/paypalSetup/image3.jpg" /></Frame>

### Step 4

Decide where you want to install PayPal, whether for a particular event or for your teams. In this instance, we will select a specific account.

<Frame><img src="/images/paypalSetup/image4.jpg" /></Frame>

### Step 5

Enter your client ID as required.

<Frame><img src="/images/paypalSetup/image5.jpg" /></Frame>

### Step 6

Provide the client's secret key.

<Frame><img src="/images/paypalSetup/image6.jpg" /></Frame>

### Step 7

Click the "Save" button to proceed.

<Frame><img src="/images/paypalSetup/image7.jpg" /></Frame>

### Step 8

Once PayPal is installed, choose the specific event app where you wish to set up PayPal by clicking on the event.

<Frame><img src="/images/paypalSetup/image8.jpg" /></Frame>

### Step 9

Navigate to the integration section.

<Frame><img src="/images/paypalSetup/image9.jpg" /></Frame>

### Step 10

Within PayPal settings, enter the price for your event.

<Frame><img src="/images/paypalSetup/image10.jpg" /></Frame>

### Step 11

For example, enter $10 and select the appropriate currency.

<Frame><img src="/images/paypalSetup/image11.jpg" /></Frame>

### Step 12

Click "Save" to confirm your settings.

<Frame><img src="/images/paypalSetup/image12.jpg" /></Frame>

### Step 13

Return to the main screen to review your payment settings.

<Frame><img src="/images/paypalSetup/image13.jpg" /></Frame>

### Step 14

Congratulations! Your setup is complete. You can now see a $10 tag on your event type.

<Frame><img src="/images/paypalSetup/image14.jpg" /></Frame>
