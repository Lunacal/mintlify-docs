## Step 1

Begin by navigating to the integration section to set up PayPal for your events.

<Frame><img src="/images/paypal_setup/image1.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 2

Select the "Payment" option.

<Frame><img src="/images/paypal_setup/image2.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 3

Proceed by clicking the "Install" button.

<Frame><img src="/images/paypal_setup/image3.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 4

Decide where you want to install PayPal, whether for a particular event or for your teams. In this instance, we will select a specific account.

<Frame><img src="/images/paypal_setup/image4.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 5

Enter your client ID & Secret Key, Click the "Save" button to proceed.

<Frame><img src="/images/paypal_setup/image7.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 6

To Get a PayPal Client ID and Secret Key, Signup/Login to a PayPal business account then goto PayPal Developer Dashboard.

<Frame><img src="/images/paypal_setup/1.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 8

Go to "Apps and Credentials" Section and Click on Create App.

<Frame><img src="/images/paypal_setup/2.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 9

Enter the Name e.g. lunacal.ai and Click on Create App.

<Frame><img src="/images/paypal_setup/3.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 10

You'll see your credentials below

<Frame><img src="/images/paypal_setup/4.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 11

Once PayPal is installed, choose the specific event app where you wish to set up PayPal by clicking on the event.

<Frame><img src="/images/paypal_setup/image8.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 12

Navigate to the integrations section.

<Frame><img src="/images/paypal_setup/image9.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 13

Enable the "PayPal" button.

<Frame><img src="/images/paypal_setup/image10.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 14

Enter the price for your event, e.g. Enter $10 and select the currency.

<Frame><img src="/images/paypal_setup/image11.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 15

Click "Save" to confirm your settings.

<Frame><img src="/images/paypal_setup/image12.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 16

Return to the main screen to review your payment settings.

<Frame><img src="/images/paypal_setup/image13.webp" /></Frame>

## Step 17

Congratulations! Your setup is complete. You can now see a $10 tag on your event type.

<Frame><img src="/images/paypal_setup/image14.webp" /></Frame>
