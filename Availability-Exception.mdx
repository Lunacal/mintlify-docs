---
title: "Availability Exception"
description: "Learn how to set availability exceptions for specific dates and times in your events."
mode: "wide"
---
Step 1  
Go to "Event Types" in your dashboard.  

<Frame><img src="/images/Availability-Exception/AV1.webp" /></Frame>

---

Step 2  
Select the event for which you want to set an availability exception and click on the "Settings" icon on that event card.  

<Frame><img src="/images/Availability-Exception/AV2.webp" /></Frame>

---

Step 3  
Choose "Edit" from the dropdown.  

<Frame><img src="/images/Availability-Exception/AV3.webp" /></Frame>

---

Step 4  
Click on the "Availability" section.  

<Frame><img src="/images/Availability-Exception/AV4.webp" /></Frame>

---

Step 5  
Scroll down and click on "Add exception."  

<Frame><img src="/images/Availability-Exception/AV5.webp" /></Frame>

---

Step 6  
Select the dates you want to set an exception.  

<Frame><img src="/images/Availability-Exception/AV6.webp" /></Frame>

---

Step 7  
Scroll down and set the time range you’re available for that date.  

<Frame><img src="/images/Availability-Exception/AV7.webp" /></Frame>

---

Step 8  
Click the "+" button to add multiple time ranges if you’re available at different slots during the same day.  

<Frame><img src="/images/Availability-Exception/AV8.webp" /></Frame>
