---

## Step 1

Go to "[Integrations](https://app.lunacal.ai/apps)" in your dashboard.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Zoom-Video/Z1.webp)
</Frame>

---

## Step 2

Click on "[Conferencing](https://app.lunacal.ai/apps?tab=apps&category=conferencing)"

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Zoom-Video/Z2.webp)
</Frame>

---

## Step 3

Select "**Zoom Video**" and choose whether you want to install it for:

- A team event type (e.g., Sales North America), or
- Your personal account (e.g., Prince Bhatt).

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Zoom-Video/Z3.webp)
</Frame>

---

## Step 4

Enter your **Zoom credentials** to connect your account.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Zoom-Video/Z4.webp)
</Frame>

---

## Step 5

**Important:** When connecting Zoom, make sure to select the "View user" option\
This permission allows Lunacal to access your Zoom profile and automatically create Zoom meetings for your bookings.

### **Steps to connect:**

1. Click **“I understood”** to proceed.
2. You’ll be redirected to the **Zoom authorization page**.
3. When prompted, choose **“View user”** as the permission option.
4. Click **Authorize** to complete the connection.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Zoom-Video/understood.webp)
</Frame>

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Zoom-Video/usersetting.webp)
</Frame>

Once connected, Zoom will automatically link to your account, and you’ll see a Connected status.

**Important** : For each event, you must manually select "**Zoom Video**" in the "Meeting Location" field.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Zoom-Video/Z5.webp)
</Frame>

---

## Step 6

Go to "**Event Types**" in your dashboard.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Zoom-Video/Z6.webp)
</Frame>

---

## Step 7

Select the event for which you want to enable **Zoom** and click the "**Settings icon**" on that event card.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Zoom-Video/Z7.webp)
</Frame>

---

## Step 8

Choose "**Edit**" from the dropdown menu.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Zoom-Video/Z8.webp)
</Frame>

---

## Step 9

In the "**Event Setup**" section, scroll down to find "**Meeting Location**".

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Zoom-Video/Z91.webp)
</Frame>

Select "**Zoom Video**" from the options.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Zoom-Video/Z92.webp)
</Frame>

---

## Step 10

Click "**Save**" to apply the changes.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Zoom-Video/Z10.webp)
</Frame>
