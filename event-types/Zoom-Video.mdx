mode: "wide"
---
---

## Step 1
Go to "Integrations" in your dashboard.

<Frame><img src="/images/Zoom-Video/Z1.webp" /></Frame>

---

## Step 2
Click on "Conferencing"

<Frame><img src="/images/Zoom-Video/Z2.webp" /></Frame>

---

## Step 3
Select "Zoom Video" and choose whether you want to install it for:  
- A team event type (e.g., Sales North America), or  
- Your personal account (e.g., Prince Bhatt).  

<Frame><img src="/images/Zoom-Video/Z3.webp" /></Frame>

---

## Step 4
Enter your Zoom credentials to connect your account.

<Frame><img src="/images/Zoom-Video/Z4.webp" /></Frame>

---

## Step 5
Once connected, Zoom will automatically link to your account, and youâ€™ll see a Connected status.  

Important : For each event, you must manually select "Zoom Video" in the "Meeting Location" field.  

<Frame><img src="/images/Zoom-Video/Z5.webp" /></Frame>

---

## Step 6
Go to "Event Types" in your dashboard.

<Frame><img src="/images/Zoom-Video/Z6.webp" /></Frame>

---

## Step 7
Select the event for which you want to enable Zoom and click the "Settings icon" on that event card.

<Frame><img src="/images/Zoom-Video/Z7.webp" /></Frame>

---

## Step 8
Choose "Edit" from the dropdown menu.

<Frame><img src="/images/Zoom-Video/Z8.webp" /></Frame>

---

## Step 9
In the "Event Setup" section, scroll down to find "Meeting Location".  
<Frame><img src="/images/Zoom-Video/Z91.webp" /></Frame>

Select "Zoom Video" from the options.

<Frame><img src="/images/Zoom-Video/Z92.webp" /></Frame>

---

## Step 10
Click "Save" to apply the changes.

<Frame><img src="/images/Zoom-Video/Z10.webp" /></Frame>
