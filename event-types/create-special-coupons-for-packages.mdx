## Overview

Special coupons allow you to apply discounts **only when the booking total meets a minimum price**. This is useful when you want to:

- Prevent coupons from being used on smaller or cheaper packages
- Encourage users to book **more slots** or **higher-value packages**
- Create targeted offers without creating multiple coupons

## Key Concept: Minimum Price

When creating a coupon, you can set a **Minimum Price**.

- The coupon will **only apply if the total booking amount is equal to or greater than this minimum price**.
- If the booking total is **below** the minimum price, the coupon will **not apply**.

## Important Notes

- The minimum price is checked **after slots are selected** and **before the discount is applied**.
- If the total booking amount changes and falls below the minimum price, the coupon will automatically become invalid.
- The coupon discount (percentage or flat) is applied **only when the minimum price condition is satisfied**.

## Summary

By setting a minimum price on coupons, you can control exactly **which booking sizes qualify for discounts**. This ensures coupons are used strategically and only on bookings that meet your business goals.
