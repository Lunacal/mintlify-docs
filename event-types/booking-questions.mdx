---
title: "Booking Questions"
description: "This section will teach you how to add custom questions that bookers must answer when scheduling a time with you."
mode: "wide"
---

**Step 1** 

Choose your event type and click on it.

<Frame><img src="/images/booking_questions/6fa3b3aa-e5cd-4efc-a13e-305464d32496.webp" /></Frame>

**Step 2** 

Navigate to the **Booking Questions** tab.

<Frame><img src="/images/booking_questions/af51f6fa-1256-4a9b-b0e5-1844c2e1f72d.webp" /></Frame>

**Step 3** 

Here, you're able to add or edit your booking questions.

<Frame><img src="/images/booking_questions/image1.webp" /></Frame>

**Step 4** 

You can Hide/Unhide questions by enabling/disabling the toggle button.

<Frame><img src="/images/booking_questions/hideUnhide.webp" /></Frame>

**Step 5**

You can make any question "Mandatory", by enabling "Mandatory" button.

<Frame><img src="/images/booking_questions/mandatory.webp" /></Frame>
