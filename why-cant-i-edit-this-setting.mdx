## Why is this setting locked?

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2](/images/Screenshot-2.webp)
</Frame>

In Lunacal, events can be created as **managed events**. This means:

- A team admin (or event owner) creates the event
- They assign it to team members
- Certain settings are **intentionally locked** to maintain consistency

So even though you can access the event, you may not be able to edit all settings.

## What does this mean for you?

If you're a team member:

- You can **view and use** the event
- But you can only edit settings that the owner has **allowed access to**

Locked settings will appear disabled or non-editable.

## How can I edit it?

You have two options:

### 1. Ask the event owner

Request the person who created the event to:

- Unlock the specific setting
- Or make you an editor (if applicable)

### 2. Create your own event

If you need full control:

- Create a new event under your account
- Customize all settings freely

## Why does Lunacal lock settings?

This helps teams:

- Maintain **consistent booking experience**
- Avoid accidental changes
- Ensure **standardized workflows** across members

If you're unsure who manages the event, check with your team admin or workspace owner.
