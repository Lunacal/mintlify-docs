## How to Connect

1. Go to **Integrations \> CRM \> Pipedrive**
1. Click **Connect**

   <Frame>
     ![Screenshot 2026 03 03 At 3 57 16 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-03at3.57.16PM.webp)
   </Frame>
2. Click on **Personal** **Account** (e,g. Payal Singh )

   <Frame>
     ![Screenshot 2026 03 03 At 3 57 23 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-03at3.57.23PM.webp)
   </Frame>
3. Enter **Client id** & **Secret key**
4. before clicking **SAVE** Copy Callback URL shown in attached image.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 03 At 3 57 50 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-03at3.57.50PM.webp)
</Frame>

**Paste Callback URL Here ( while you creating app inside pipedrive):**

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 03 At 3 58 03 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-03at3.58.03PM.webp)
</Frame>

**Make sure these scopes are enabled in Pipedrive:**

- Access to information
- Contacts → Full access
- Activities → Full access
- Click **SAVE**
- Setup **Completed.**

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 03 At 3 58 22 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-03at3.58.22PM.webp)
</Frame>

# How to Enable Pipedrive Inside Your Events

- Go to **Event Types**

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 03 At 3 55 49 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-03at3.55.49PM-1.webp)
</Frame>

- Click on your event
- go to **integrations**

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 03 At 3 55 56 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-03at3.55.56PM.webp)
</Frame>

- Enable **Pipedrive**

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 03 At 3 59 30 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-03at3.59.30PM.webp)
</Frame>

## Make a Booking with Custom Question

1. Open **Booking questions** inside your event.
2. Click **Add / Edit booking question**.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 03 At 3 59 57 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-03at3.59.57PM.webp)
</Frame>

3. Select **Pipedrive Custom Field**.
3. Enter **API Key** & **Setup your booking question**

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 2026 03 03 At 4 00 04 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-03at4.00.04PM.webp)
</Frame>

4. **Save** the question.
5. Open your booking link.

   <Frame>
     ![Screenshot 2026 03 03 At 4 01 33 PM](/images/Screenshot2026-03-03at4.01.33PM.png)
   </Frame>
6. Fill in the form and submit.

## New Contact Created

If the email does not exist in Pipedrive:

- A new **Person** record is automatically created.
- Name, email, phone, and mapped custom fields are populated.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot](/images/Screenshot-1.webp)
</Frame>

## Existing Contact Updated

If the email already exists:

- The existing **Person** record is updated.
