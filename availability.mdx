# Setting Up Availability

> In this guide, you will learn how to configure and manage your availability settings efficiently. The process involves creating new availability schedules, modifying existing ones, setting specific time frames, and implementing overrides for specific days.

## Step 1

### Access the Availability Section

To begin configuring your availability, navigate to the Availability section.

<Frame><img src="/images/Availability/image1.jpg" /></Frame>

## Step 2

### Create or Modify Availability

In this section, you have the option to either create a new availability schedule or modify the existing ones.

<Frame><img src="/images/Availability/image2.jpg" /></Frame>

## Step 3

### Edit Availability Name

You can change the name of your availability to something more descriptive.

<Frame><img src="/images/Availability/image3.jpg" /></Frame>

## Step 4

### Select Introduction Option

Choose the 'Introduction' option to proceed.

<Frame><img src="/images/Availability/image4.jpg" /></Frame>

## Step 5

### Adjust Time Zone and Unavailability Settings

Here, you can modify the time zone and also manage unavailability settings.

<Frame><img src="/images/Availability/image5.jpg" /></Frame>

## Step 6

### Manage Time Settings

Toggle the unavailability settings on or off, and set the specific times as needed.

<Frame><img src="/images/Availability/image6.jpg" /></Frame>

## Step 7

### Define Availability Hours

For example, set your availability from 8:00 AM to 4:00 PM.

<Frame><img src="/images/Availability/image7.jpg" /></Frame>

## Step 8

### Add Additional Time Slots

If you are available after 4:00 PM, you can add another slot, such as from 4:30 PM to 5:00 PM.

<Frame><img src="/images/Availability/image8.jpg" /></Frame>

## Step 9

### Copy Time to Other Days

Easily copy the specified time slots to the desired days.

<Frame><img src="/images/Availability/image9.jpg" /></Frame>

## Step 10

### Apply Changes

Once you have made the desired adjustments, click 'Apply' to save the changes.

<Frame><img src="/images/Availability/image10.jpg" /></Frame>

## Step 11

### Verify Day-wise Availability

You will see that the availability is set for all selected days. You can also set a day override if needed.

<Frame><img src="/images/Availability/image11.jpg" /></Frame>

## Step 12

### Add a Day Override

To add a specific day override, click on 'Add an override.'

<Frame><img src="/images/Availability/image12.jpg" /></Frame>

## Step 13

### Implement All-day Unavailability

Suppose you are unavailable all day on a certain date; you can add this as an override.

<Frame><img src="/images/Availability/image13.jpg" /></Frame>

## Step 14

### Confirm Override Implementation

You will see that the override has been successfully implemented.

<Frame><img src="/images/Availability/image14.jpg" /></Frame>

## Step 15

### Save Your Settings

Click on 'Save' to finalize your settings.

<Frame><img src="/images/Availability/image15.jpg" /></Frame>

## Step 16

### Confirm Saved Availability

Your availability settings are now saved successfully.

<Frame><img src="/images/Availability/image16.jpg" /></Frame>

## Step 17

### Duplicate or Delete Availability

If needed, you can duplicate your availability settings or delete them by selecting the appropriate options.
