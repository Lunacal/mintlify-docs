## What is Lunacal?

Lunacal is a modern scheduling platform built to simplify bookings and elevate your brand. Create elegant, fully customizable booking pages where clients can easily schedule time with you — and where you can showcase your value.

Add testimonials, videos, and custom text content directly to your scheduling page to build trust, share your story, and convert more visitors into bookings.

Our mission is to help individuals and businesses save time, reduce no-shows, and stay organized — all through an intuitive, powerful scheduling experience designed for the way you work.
