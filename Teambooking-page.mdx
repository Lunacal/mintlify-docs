## Step 1

Click "Edit Team Booking" Page from the top-right corner.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/booking_page/book1.webp)
</Frame>

---

## Step 2

### Book a Member Directly

This section displays all team members you’ve added.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/booking_page/book2.webp)
</Frame>

Visitors can click on an individual member to book directly with them.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/booking_page/book3.webp)
</Frame>

You can hide any member by clicking the eye icon (visibility toggle) shown on their profile card.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/booking_page/book4.webp)
</Frame>

---

## Step 3

### Event Types

- This block shows all the event types created for the team.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/booking_page/book5.webp)
</Frame>

- Visitors can click on an individual event to book directly.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/booking_page/book6.webp)
</Frame>

- You can hide any event by clicking the eye icon (visibility toggle) shown on the event card.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/booking_page/book7.webp)
</Frame>

---

## Step 4

### Widgets & Customization-

You can add extra widgets such as About Me, Video Embed, FAQs, or Why Us? to personalize the page.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/booking_page/book8.webp)
</Frame>

---

## Step 5

Important: After making any changes, don’t forget to click "Publish" (top-right corner) so updates go live.
