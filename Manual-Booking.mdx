---
title: "Manual Booking"
description: "Learn how to enable manual booking approval for your events."
mode: "wide"
---

Step 1\
Go to "Event Types" in your dashboard.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Manual-Booking/MBL1.webp)
</Frame>

Step 2\
Select the event for which you want to enable manual booking approval and click on the "Settings" icon on that event card.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Manual-Booking/MBL2.webp)
</Frame>

---

Step 3\
Choose "Edit" from the dropdown.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Manual-Booking/MBL3.webp)
</Frame>

---

Step 4\
In the "Event Types" section, scroll down to find "Advanced."

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Manual-Booking/MBL4.webp)
</Frame>

---

Step 5\
In "Advanced" scroll down and toggle on "Approve bookings manually."

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Manual-Booking/MBL5.webp)
</Frame>

---

Step 6\
Click "Save" to apply the changes.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Manual-Booking/MBL6.webp)
</Frame>
