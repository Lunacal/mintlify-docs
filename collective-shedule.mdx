---
title: "Collective Scheduling"
description: "Learn how to use Collective Scheduling so events are booked only when all selected team members are available."
---

## Step 1

Click on "Create a new event".

<Frame>
  ![](/images/collective-shedule/col1.webp)
</Frame>

---

## Step 2

Select "Collective Scheduling" from the Assignment options.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/collective-shedule/col2.webp)
</Frame>

---

## Step 3

The Event Setup, Booking Limits, and other features work the same way as a regular event.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/collective-shedule/col3.webp)
</Frame>

---

## Step 4

Go to the Assignment section.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/collective-shedule/col4.webp)
</Frame>

---

## Step 5

### Default Hosts

- Turn ON "Add all current & future team members" → Automatically include everyone in all meetings.

  <Frame>
    ![](/images/collective-shedule/col5.webp)
  </Frame>
- Keep it OFF → Manually select the specific team members you want to always include in the meeting.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/collective-shedule/col6.webp)
</Frame>

---

## Step 6

### Availability Options

- Choose a Common Schedule (ON): All hosts follow the same availability schedule you set here. Any changes apply to all linked events.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/collective-shedule/col7.webp)
</Frame>

- Choose a Common Schedule (OFF):Each host can set and use their own individual availability for events.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/collective-shedule/col8.webp)
</Frame>
