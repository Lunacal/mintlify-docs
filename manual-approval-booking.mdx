---
title: "Manual Booking Approval"
description: "Learn how to enable manual booking approval for your events."
mode: "wide"
---

When you enable **Manual Booking**, all booking requests for this Event Type will require your approval before they are confirmed.
This gives you full control over who gets approved, ideal for high-value sessions, limited seats, or situations where you want to screen attendees before confirming.

when **Manual Booking** is turned on:
- Client sends a booking request
- The booking stays in Pending state
- You (the host) must **manually Approve** or **Reject** it from email and [bookings section](https://app.lunacal.ai/bookings/upcoming)
- Only after approval the client receives the confirmation email & calendar invite
- This prevents unwanted or spam bookings from being auto-confirmed.

Step 1\
Go to "[Event Types](https://app.lunacal.ai/event-types)" in your dashboard.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Manual-Booking/MBL1.webp)
</Frame>

Step 2\
Select the event for which you want to enable manual booking approval and click on the **Settings** icon on that event card.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Manual-Booking/MBL2.webp)
</Frame>

---

Step 3\
Choose **Edit** from the dropdown.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Manual-Booking/MBL3.webp)
</Frame>

---

Step 4\
In the "**Event Types**" section, scroll down to find "**Advanced.**"

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Manual-Booking/MBL4.webp)
</Frame>

---

Step 5\
In "**Advanced**" scroll down and toggle on "**Approve bookings manually.**"

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Manual-Booking/MBL5.webp)
</Frame>

---

Step 6\
Click "**Save**" to apply the changes.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/Manual-Booking/MBL6.webp)
</Frame>
