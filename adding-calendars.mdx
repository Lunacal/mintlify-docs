## Step 1

Begin by navigating to your settings.

<Frame><img src="/images/adding-calendars/1.webp" /></Frame>

---

## Step 2

Click on the **Calendar** option.

<Frame><img src="/images/adding-calendars/2.webp" /></Frame>

You'll notice that there's no calendar connected at this point.

---

## Step 3

Click on **Connect**.

<Frame><img src="/images/adding-calendars/3.webp" /></Frame>

---

## Step 4

Choose **Calendar Apps**. For this tutorial, we're connecting a Google Calendar. Click on **Install**.

<Frame><img src="/images/adding-calendars/4.webp" /></Frame>

---

## Step 5

Select your Google account.

<Frame><img src="/images/adding-calendars/5.webp" /></Frame>

---

## Step 6

Click on **Continue**.



Your calendar is now connected. You'll see that events are being added to this calendar.

---

## Step 7

If you have multiple calendars, you're able to change the calendar where events are added. For example, if you have another calendar named "Calendar 2.0", you can switch to this one.


Click to change the calendar where events are added.


You'll see that the calendar for adding events has now changed to "Calendar 2.0".

---

## Step 8

You also have the option to add other calendars. For example, you might want to add another Google Calendar or install new calendar apps like Apple Calendar or Outlook Calendar.
