title: "Redirect Attendees to a URL After Booking"
description: "Learn how to automatically redirect attendees to a specific URL once their booking is confirmed."
mode: "wide"
---

When a attendee completes a booking, lunacal allows you to automatically redirect them to any URL you choose — for example, a custom “Thank You” page, or a confirmation screen on your own website.

## Step 1  
Go to [Event Type](https://app.lunacal.ai/event-types) in your dashboard.  

<Frame><img src="/images/Redirect-url/URL1.webp" /></Frame>

---

## Step 2  
Select the event for which you want to set up a redirect after booking and click on the Settings (⚙️) icon on that event card.  
<Frame><img src="/images/Redirect-url/URL2.webp" /></Frame>

---

## Step 3  
Choose "**Edit**" from the dropdown.  

<Frame><img src="/images/Redirect-url/URL3.webp" /></Frame>

---

## Step 4  
In the "**Event Types**" section, scroll down to find "**Advanced.**"  

<Frame><img src="/images/Redirect-url/URL4.webp" /></Frame>

---

## Step 5  
Toggle ON "**Send to a URL after booking.**"  
<Frame><img src="/images/Redirect-url/URL6.webp" /></Frame>

---

## Step 6  
**Enter** or **Paste** the link you want attendees to be redirected to after booking is confirmed.  

## Examples
- https://yourwebsite.com/thank-you
- https://yourwebsite.com/instructions?utm_source=lunacal
- https://payment-page.com/checkout

<Frame><img src="/images/Redirect-url/URL6.webp" /></Frame>

---

## Step 7  
Click "**Save**" to apply the changes.  

<Frame><img src="/images/Redirect-url/URL7.webp" /></Frame>
